Pros&Cons lets you make any decision you need by helping you build a pro and a con list. You are also able to view public lists organized by topic, tags, hashtags. You may start a new public list to get people to fill both your Pro and Con list with what they think. Collaborative, innovative! Making a decision was never so easy! If you are a registered user you can make private lists, or even allow only the people you want access to your lists. You may use it in your company as well! No more paper for this matter! There will be two categories of users: normal user, who can make public lists that anyone can edit, or private lists that nobody else can edit. And there is also premium user, who can make private collaborative lists.

Backers will have access to premium accounts for life! That is true for Pro&Cons and also any project derived from Pro&Cons (we have some amazing ideas in our pockets!)! Re-imagine the way you decide things by visualizing all alternatives! With a premium account will be able to upload images, draw graphs, diagrams, and more!

FAQ

Why do I need to make a list? I could just flip a coin, couldn't I?
By making a list you visualize and let your BRAIN decide! This way no emotions are involved and you make the BEST decision you could make! And yes, you could just flip a coin, but that you be just luck (or bad luck), wouldn't it? =)
Why do you need money?
Despite looking something so simple, money is required to build the whole infrastructure to back all this up. Pros&Cons won't be just a dumb website. It will sugest topics for each list you make, guessing by title, topics you've written, images, etc. All that require work and effort!
